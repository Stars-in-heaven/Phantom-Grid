// What Is Phantom-Grid

Built for
the Field.

Phantom-Grid is a series of purpose-built handheld devices engineered for those who operate outside the comfort of connectivity. No Wi-Fi. No cloud sync. No IP assignment.

Every hardware and firmware decision is governed by a single principle: security first, convenience never.

"Inconvenience is the mechanism by which Phantom-Grid remains undetectable, untrackable, and uncompromised."
